I’d like to live with You...

Marina Tsvetaeva

  1. I’d like to live with You

  2. In a small town,

  3. Where there are eternal twilights

  4. And eternal bells.

  5. And in a small village inn —

  6. The faint chime

  7. Of ancient clocks — like droplets of time.

  8. And sometimes, in the evenings, from some garret —

  9. A flute,

  10. And the flautist himself in the window.

  11. And big tulips in the window-sills.

  12. And maybe, You would not even love me…

  13. In the middle of the room — a huge tiled oven,

  14. On each tile — a small picture:

  15. A rose — a heart — a ship. —

  16. And in the one window —

  17. Snow, snow, snow.

  18. You would lie — thus I love You: idle,

  19. Indifferent, carefree.

  20. Now and then the sharp strike

  21. Of a match.

  22. The cigarette glows and burns down,

  23. And trembles for a long, long time on its edge

  24. In a gray brief pillar — of ash.

  25. You’re too lazy even to flick it —

  26. And the whole cigarette flies into the fire.
